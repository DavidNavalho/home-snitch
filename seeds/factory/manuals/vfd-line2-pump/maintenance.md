# ABB ACS580 Preventive Maintenance Schedule

## Daily

- Verify the assistant panel shows `RDY` (ready) with no warning chip.
- Confirm cooling fans are spinning and the cabinet vents are unobstructed.

## Monthly

- Vacuum cabinet vents; check filter media and replace if greater than 50% blocked.
- Verify torque on input and motor cable terminations to 17 N·m.

## Annually

- Capture the parameter backup via Drive Composer Pro.
- Run a self-test from the operator panel. Record the result in the maintenance log.

## Stocked Spare Parts

- Cooling fan kit `BFAN-580-A2`
- IGBT module `BIGBT-580-11`
- Operator panel `ACS-AP-W` keypad
