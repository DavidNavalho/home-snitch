# Cognex IS-2000 Preventive Maintenance

## Daily

- Verify the PASS rate is greater than 99.5% over the last shift.
- Wipe the lens cover with a microfiber cloth.

## Weekly

- Run the **Calibrate** job. Record the calibration score in the line log.
- Confirm trigger sensor mounting is unchanged.

## Annually

- Back up the In-Sight job to the maintenance vault via In-Sight Explorer.
- Inspect the C-mount lens lock screws.
