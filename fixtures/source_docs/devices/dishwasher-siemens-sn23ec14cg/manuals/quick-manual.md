## Troubleshooting

### Error Codes

#### E24

E24 means the drain system is blocked or water cannot drain correctly in this fixture.

Safe guidance: check the official manual and contact Siemens service if the fault remains. Do not infer another dishwasher model's error-code guidance for this Siemens fixture.
