# Bosch SMS6ZCW00G Quick Manual Fixture

This offline fixture represents a short manual excerpt for deterministic tests.

## Safety

Use the official manual before acting on appliance faults. If a water-related fault appears, avoid running the dishwasher until the source manual or service advice confirms it is safe.

## Troubleshooting

### Error Codes

#### E15

E15 means the water protection system has been activated or water has been detected in the base area.

Safe guidance: turn off the water supply if it is safe to do so, check the official manual, and contact Bosch service. This fixture does not provide repair or disassembly instructions.

#### E09

E09 indicates a heating circuit fault in this fixture. Contact service rather than assuming a user-serviceable repair.
